Welcome to Graph Methods in TDA 2026

This workshop brings together researchers working at the intersection of graph methods and topological data analysis (TDA). We aim to foster connections between theory and applications, spanning pure mathematics, computer science, and data science.

We welcome participants from all career stages, from graduate students to senior researchers.

Topics of interest include (but are not limited to):

  • Persistent homology on graphs and networks
  • Reeb graphs and Mapper algorithms
  • Graph neural networks with topological features
  • Applications to biological, social, and sensor networks
  • Theoretical foundations of graph-based TDA
