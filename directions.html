By Air

The closest major airports are:

  • Lansing Capital Region International (LAN) — approximately 15 minutes from campus
  • Detroit Metropolitan Airport (DTW) — approximately 90 minutes from campus

Rental cars are available at both airports. Shuttle service from DTW is available via Michigan Flyer.

By Car

MSU is located in East Lansing, MI, accessible via I-96 and US-127. Visitor parking is available — see MSU Parking Services for maps and rates.
