## By Air

The closest major airports are:

- **Lansing Capital Region International Airport (LAN)** — ~15 min from campus
- **Detroit Metropolitan Airport (DTW)** — ~90 min from campus

Rental cars are available at both airports. Shuttle service from DTW is also available via [Michigan Flyer](https://michiganflyer.com).
